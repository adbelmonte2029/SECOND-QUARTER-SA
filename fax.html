Facts and Demographics

Old Fort Bonifacio, now BGC

Originally, before developed BGC was previously known as Fort Bonifacio, an American military base.

Showcases Public Art

BGC stands out for embedding art and science intro people's everyday life, by featuring public galleries, and institutions such as The Mind Museum.

The Ideal Location

Its location, near other major businesses such as Makati, makes it highly accessible and a great location for businesses and residents.

Urban Design

It features parks, open spaces, and public art. Recognized for its urban design, pedestrian-friendly streets, and lush parks.

World-class Quality!

BGC is known for world-class shopping malls, trendy restaurants, and vibrant nightlife.

"Wall Street of Asia"

Has been called the "Wall Street of Asia" due to its status as a leading financial and corporate center here in the Philippines.

Huge Daytime Influx

The population of workers during the day increases significantly and drops at night, showing its core function as a workplace.

High Socio-Economic Stats

Residents and workers in the area, mostly have high education levels and have above average incomes.

Young Residents

The residence is primarily composed of young professionals with a high tendency to rent, rather than own. Leading to small household sizes.
